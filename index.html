The next step in wind power

A unique design

Our wind turbines are specifically engineered and designed for urban areas. Since the very first sketches, installing our product on the roof of houses and on balconies was part of the plan.

Lightweight and recyclable

The blades of our wind turbines are made from a very lightweight material, and every single part is recyclable. Its modular conception makes it easy to replace a broken piece.

Grid-Synced Inverter

Every wind turbine comes with a grid-synced inverter. This means that you can still use the grid's electricity, and resell your surplus.

Savings from everywhere

Half the price

Because we build every part ourselves, we can lower the cost of our wind turbine to be half of current offerings.

Zero maintenance costs

Every part is recyclable, which equals to a near-zero maintenance costs for you!

Reselling power surplus

With the Grid-Synced inverter, you can resell your electricity surplus to the grid. It's like getting paid to go outside! 🎉
